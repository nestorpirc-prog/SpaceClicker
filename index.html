About the Game

Space Clicker is a relaxing but addictive space-themed clicking game where every click generates cosmic energy. Travel through the galaxy, discover mysterious planets, and build your own interstellar economy.

The game begins with a simple click, but quickly evolves into a deep cosmic adventure where you unlock powerful upgrades, explore distant worlds, and automate your energy production.

Upgrade your space stations, collect rare cosmic particles, and expand your influence across the universe. Each upgrade makes your energy production faster, bringing you closer to becoming the ultimate space tycoon.

Whether you enjoy casual clicker games or love futuristic space adventures, Space Clicker delivers a satisfying and visually immersive experience.
