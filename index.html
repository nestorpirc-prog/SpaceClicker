About The Game

Space Clicker is a relaxing yet incredibly addictive cosmic clicking game where every click generates powerful stellar energy.

Start with a simple planet and transform your small space station into a massive interstellar civilization powered by cosmic energy.

Unlock planets, discover mysterious galaxies, upgrade futuristic technologies and expand your influence across the universe.

With smooth animations, beautiful cosmic visuals and satisfying progression, Space Clicker creates a relaxing but exciting space exploration experience.
