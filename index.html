About the Game

SpaceClicker is a simple but fun space-themed clicker game where you collect energy by clicking on planets. Your mission is to explore space, gather resources, and become the most powerful explorer in the galaxy.

Each click gives you energy that you can use to progress further and discover more of the universe. The game is designed to be relaxing and satisfying while giving players a small taste of cosmic exploration.

Whether you want to play for a few minutes or spend time collecting massive amounts of energy, SpaceClicker offers a simple but addictive gameplay experience.
