About the Game

SpaceClicker is a simple and relaxing space themed clicker game where you collect energy and explore the endless universe. Your goal is to travel through space, discover planets, and become the greatest explorer in the galaxy.

The game focuses on simple gameplay that anyone can enjoy. Tap to collect energy, progress through space, and unlock new discoveries as you continue your journey through the stars.

SpaceClicker is designed for quick fun sessions while still giving players a sense of exploration and progress in a mysterious cosmic world.
