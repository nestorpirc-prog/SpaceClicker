About the Game

SpaceClicker is a relaxing space themed clicker game where you collect energy and explore the universe. Travel between planets, gather resources, and expand your cosmic adventure.

The game is designed for quick and fun gameplay sessions. Tap, progress, and discover more of the galaxy as you continue your journey through space.

Perfect for players who enjoy simple but satisfying gameplay with a cosmic atmosphere.
