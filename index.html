About The Game

Space Clicker is a relaxing but incredibly addictive space-themed clicking game where every click generates cosmic energy.

In the beginning you simply click a planet to generate energy, but as the game progresses you unlock powerful upgrades, discover mysterious planets, and build advanced interstellar technology.

Travel across galaxies, expand your energy production, and transform a tiny cosmic station into a massive space civilization powered by infinite stellar energy.

With smooth animations, atmospheric visuals, and satisfying gameplay progression, Space Clicker delivers a unique experience for both casual players and hardcore clicker fans.
