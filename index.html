About the Game

Space Clicker is a relaxing yet addictive cosmic clicking game. With every click you generate stellar energy which allows you to upgrade your space technology and explore deeper regions of the universe.

Discover mysterious planets, unlock powerful cosmic upgrades, and build a futuristic interstellar civilization powered by infinite cosmic energy.

Designed with smooth animations and atmospheric visuals, Space Clicker offers an immersive experience where every click brings you closer to controlling the galaxy.
